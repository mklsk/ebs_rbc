  • Rostislav Ordovsky-Tanaevsky Blanco is the first Russian businessman to be awarded Person of the Year annual national award in the field of business twice: in 2000 and in 2002.

  • One of the most influential media managers in Russia and the current CEO of RBC - Russia’s leading business media

Gene Moldavsky

Marat Guelman

Igor Vittel
